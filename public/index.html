Join us For Church

We believe in the power of corporate and individual prayer.

Sunday Pre-Service Prayer 10:30-11am

Sunday service 11-12:30am

On Sundays, we meet in the sanctuary before each service to pray together. This moment allows individuals and families to ready themselves and invite the Holy Spirit through Intercessory prayers as we prepare for the move of God through out the service.

Friday Prayer service at 7-8:30pm

On Friday Nights, we assemble in the sanctuary from 7:00pm to 8:30pm for corporate prayer. This is a remarkable time spent in God's presence. We gather to seek the Lord and call upon His Name. The evening starts with an exhortation or testimony, leading us into our prayer session. At the close of our prayer time, the individual who shared at the start of the evening concludes with a closing prayer.

Our Belief:The Beginning

Genesis 1:1 (NKJV) “In the beginning God created…” God is an architect, a builder, and has been so since the beginning of time. He is the Creator and Originator of all things. The sun moon and stars, the waters and the earth, every living creature according to its kind, humanity: God is the Creator of all.

Humanity

Humanity was created in the likeness and image of God. Genesis 1:26 (NKJV) Then God said, “Let Us make man in Our image, according to Our likeness.” Man was created to have eternal fellowship and relationship with God, but became separated in that relationship as a result of sinful disobedience (Genesis 3). Man cannot regain right relationship with God by his own efforts. Our only hope is Jesus Christ.
Salvation Prayer: Dear Jesus, thank you for dying on the Cross for me. Thank you for paying the price for my sin.Today I turn from my sins and ask for Your forgiveness. I acknowledge You as Lord and Saviour of my life. I commit my life into Your hands. Amen.

Through accepting Jesus as your personal Saviour and Lord, you can know and experience God’s love and plan for your life.

Acts 2:38 (NKJV) Then Peter said to them, “Repent, and let every one of you be baptized in the name of Jesus Christ for the remission of sins; and you shall receive the gift of the Holy Spirit.”
Repentance:Repentance: A 180 degree turn – from one’s own way to God’s way, a complete change of direction, a decision to follow Christ.Water Baptism: Identification with Christ in death, burial and resurrection; the burying of the “old man” (the former sinful life) in a watery grave and the raising up of the “new man” to walk in the power of Christ.Holy Spirit Baptism : A personal Pentecost, an Acts 2:4 encounter, an experience with the Holy Spirit subsequent to salvation complete with the evidence of speaking in tongues.

Trinity

God exists in perfect unity within Himself. He exists as one God in three persons: the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit. Although each member of the Trinity serves different functions, they are One. 1 John 5:7 (NKJV) For there are three that bear witness in heaven: the Father, the Word [Jesus Christ], and the Holy Spirit; and these three are one.

The Bible

The Bible is the Living Word of God. The Bible was written by human writers under the direct inspiration of the Holy Spirit (God). The Bible is God’s Word to every living person. Within its pages lies humanity’s hope. The answers are found in the Bible, from Genesis to Revelation. We find wisdom and understanding, correction and direction, all the answers to life in this one Book. The Bible is a manual for daily living – a manual for a lifetime. Immerse yourself in the story of God’s incredible grace. Read of ordinary men and women living extraordinary lives. Read of their faith, their trials, their courage, their surrender; their journey with God. The Bible is a book that will change your life.

Devotions

Christianity is a relationship. Every day we should set time aside to spend time with God. Personal devotions, family devotions; God is longing to commune with us. Take time each day to pray, worship, and read the Bible.

Our Values

  • Teachable: We value the spirit of humility.
  • Thankfulness: We consider kingdom involvement a privilege.
